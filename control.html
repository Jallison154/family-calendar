üìπ Camera Feeds

‚ñº

Camera Feeds (RTSP/ONVIF/MJPEG/HLS/Scrypted)
Various camera streaming methods are supported:

  • Scrypted HLS Rebroadcast (recommended, no auth): https://scrypted-ip:10443/endpoint/@scrypted/rebroadcast/hls/26
  • Scrypted WebRTC (low latency, may need auth): https://scrypted-ip:10443/webrtc/camera-name
  • Scrypted Public Page (requires authentication): https://scrypted-ip:10443/endpoint/@scrypted/core/public/#/device/26
  • MJPEG (works everywhere): http://camera-ip/mjpg/video.mjpg or http://camera-ip/video.cgi
  • HLS: http://camera-ip/stream.m3u8
  • Snapshots: http://camera-ip/snapshot.cgi
  • RTSP: Requires server-side conversion to HLS (needs ffmpeg)
Note: If you see "Not logged in" errors with Scrypted public pages, use the HLS rebroadcast URL instead.
